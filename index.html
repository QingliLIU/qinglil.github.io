About

I am Qingli Liu. I work on AI-driven accident investigation and safety analytics, with a focus on agentic and interpretable systems for causal chain reconstruction in safety-critical domains (e.g., aviation and transportation).

Research Interests

  • Agentic AI for accident investigation (evidence integration, causal DAG reconstruction)
  • Human–AI interaction in safety-critical settings (trust, workload, mismatch)
  • Interpretable causal representations and safety decision support
